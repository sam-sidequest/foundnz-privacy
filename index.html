Privacy Policy

Found NZ — Effective Date: June 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Found NZ. Found NZ is a mobile application that helps people discover activities across New Zealand. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

By using Found NZ, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions, you can contact us at the email address listed at the bottom of this document.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

Found NZ requests access to your device's location (GPS) solely to calculate approximate drive times from your current location to activities near you. Location data is:

  • Used only in the moment to generate drive time estimates
  • Never stored on our servers
  • Never shared with third parties beyond what is necessary for drive time calculation (via Google Maps APIs)
  • Only accessed when you have granted permission through your device settings

2.2 App Preferences

Found NZ stores your in-app preferences (such as your selected moods, activity types, transport options, and age groups) locally on your device using iOS secure storage (AsyncStorage). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not linked to any personal identity
  • Can be cleared at any time by deleting the app

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

Found NZ does not collect or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information (we have no user accounts)
  • Precise or historical location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioural tracking data
  • Payment information
  • Any data from children under 13

3. How We Use Information

The limited information accessed by Found NZ is used exclusively to provide app functionality:

  • Location access is used to estimate drive times from your current position to activities
  • On-device preferences are used to personalise activity recommendations in the "For You" feed

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond core app functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

Found NZ uses Google Maps APIs (Directions API and Geocoding API) to calculate drive time estimates. When drive times are requested, your approximate location may be transmitted to Google as part of this calculation. Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

We do not use any other third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

5. Data Storage and Security

Found NZ does not operate servers that store personal data. All preference data is stored locally on your device. Drive time calculations are performed in real time and no location data is retained after the calculation is complete.

Your device's built-in security protections (encryption, passcode, Face ID/Touch ID) protect any locally stored preference data.

6. Children's Privacy

Found NZ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us so we can address the issue promptly.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Location Permissions

You can withdraw location access at any time through your iPhone's Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Found NZ. The app will continue to function without location access, though drive time estimates will not be available.

7.2 Clearing Preferences

Your on-device preferences can be cleared by uninstalling the app. There is no account to delete because we do not create accounts.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Found NZ after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
